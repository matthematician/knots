An example of a ternary operation on the set \(S = \mathbb{R}^3\) of vectors in 3-space is the vector sum:

\begin{equation*} [ -,-,- ] \colon \mathbb{R}^3\times\mathbb{R}^3\times\mathbb{R}^3 \to \mathbb{R}^3, \qquad ({\bf u}, {\bf v},{\bf w}) \mapsto {\bf u} + {\bf v} + {\bf w}. \end{equation*}
