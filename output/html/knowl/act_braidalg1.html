The Sage cell below instantiates the positive braids in \(B_4\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} u = s_2s_1s_3s_2 \quad v = s_1s_3 \quad w = s_1^{-1}s_3^{-1}. \end{equation*}

Use it to do the following.

  1. Generate a diagram of the braid \(vw = s_1s_3s_1^{-1}s_3^{-1}\text{.}\)

  2. Ask Sage to test whether \(vw = e\) using the command

    v*w == e
    (note the double-equal sign.

  3. How do you reconcile the answers to parts (a) and (b)?

  4. Determine an inverse for the braid \(u\text{,}\) and compare your result to your answers above.
