Activity 2.1.7.

  1. Sketch two braids \(b_1,b_2 \in B_4\) of your choosing. Use a diagram to explain why \(F(b_1b_2) = F(b_1) \circ F(b_2)\text{.}\)

  2. For the anagram \(\sigma \colon SHALE \mapsto LEASH\) as a permutation in \(S_5\text{,}\) use a braid-a-gram to determine a braid \(b \in B_5\) for which \(F(b) = \sigma\text{.}\) Which property in Definition 2.1.12 does your reasoning seem to verify or disprove?

  3. Sketch two different braids \(b_1,b_2 \in B_3\) for which \(F(b_1) = \epsilon \in S_3\) and \(F(b_2) = \epsilon \in S_3\text{.}\) Which property in Definition 2.1.12 does your reasoning seem to verify or disprove?
