An example of a binary operation on the set \(S = \mathbb{Q}\) of integers is the multiplication operation:

\begin{equation*} \cdot \colon \mathbb{Q}\times\mathbb{Q} \to \mathbb{Q}, \qquad (x,y) \mapsto x\cdot y. \end{equation*}

Under this operation, for example, \((\frac15, -20) \mapsto -4\) and \((-\frac25, -\frac52) \mapsto 1.\)
