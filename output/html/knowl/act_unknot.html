Activity 3.1.2.

Physically construct an unknot \(U\) using rope or string.

Then, review your work from Activity 2.1.2 and/or Activity 2.1.6 to inspire the following.

  1. Perform an isotopy on your knot to discover and sketch a diagram of \(U\) having exactly one crossing.

  2. Perform an isotopy on your knot to discover and sketch a diagram of \(U\) having exactly two crossings.

  3. How would you describe the isotopy that established the braid relation (2.1.2) in Activity 2.1.2? Demonstrate how this might work with your unknot, by first creating a diagram of \(U\) having at least three crossings.
